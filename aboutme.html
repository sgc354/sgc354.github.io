Why business?

Going into university, I thought I would be a pre-med student. However, something about NYU Stern’s unique qualities piqued my attention - especially its New York City location and flexible study abroad opportunities.

When I got in, I thought that I would still follow the pre-med track while pursuing my B.S in business. However, I realized that what drew me to medicine in the first place wasn’t medicine itself - it was the chance to use my skills to help people. One of my mentors (with a background in finance, but who now works in product management) once told me that once you fall in love with the problem, any path you use to solve it will be interesting for you.

I believe my true passion lies in social impact. Now the question is how I can marry it with business: whether through data analysis, tech for social impact, or environmental sustainability.
